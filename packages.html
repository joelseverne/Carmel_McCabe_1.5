Wedding Photography Packages

Every wedding day is important, personal and individual, so its only fair that it is photographed in the perfect way to suit you and your needs.
I love working with couples to ensure a tailor made service that suits everyone involved. I am more than happy to offer no obligation quotes and consultations to help you with planning and budgeting

Full wedding day coverage includes photography of all your morning preparations, including venue set up and those all important behind the scenes up until the first dance.
You also have the option of a half day service, ideal for those on a tighter budget, but still wanting to capture the most important parts of the day.

What's included:

  • Rustic presentation box
  • A set of fully edited high res images presented on a USB
  • A set of compressed images ideal for social media
  • A selection of five 4X6 prints (because hard copies are exciting!)
  • A small token of congratulations!

Optional extras:

  • Engagement shoots (FREE WITH ALL WEDDING PACKAGE BOOKINGS!)
  • Anniversary shoots
  • Wedding album curation
