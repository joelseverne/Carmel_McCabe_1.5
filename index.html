Let me introduce myself;
my name is Carmel Teresa McCabe.

I’m a photographer based in South Wales, inspired by people, places and above all things, love!

For several years now I have been working in various fields of photography, both comercially and personally. Since the planning of my own wedding, my love of love knows no bounds! I truly feel so privileged and honored to share in the joy of so many peoples wedding day.

Combining my passion of creating beautiful imagery and celebrating love!

My aim is not to only capture those raw emotions, real reactions and defining moments of your day, but to also act as friendly support and help you to live out the day of your dreams! Lets do this!
