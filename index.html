Let me introduce myself;
my name is Carmel Teresa McCabe.

I’m a photographer based in South Wales, inspired by people, places and above all things, love!

I truly feel so privileged and honored to share in the joy of so many peoples wedding day. Combining my passion of creating beautiful imagery and celebrating love!
